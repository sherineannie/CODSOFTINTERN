VARIETIES

Apple

Apples are crisp and refreshing fruits, known for their sweet-tart flavor and a wide variety of colors, such as red, green, and yellow. They're a healthy snack choice and a versatile ingredient in both sweet and savory dishes.

Orange

Oranges are vibrant and juicy citrus fruits, prized for their zesty, tangy flavor and rich vitamin C content. They make for a delightful, energizing addition to your daily fruit intake.

Guva

Guavas are tropical gems, known for their fragrant aroma and sweet, slightly grainy flesh. Packed with vitamin C and dietary fiber, they offer a delightful and nutritious taste of the tropics.

Grape

Grapes are nature's bite-sized jewels, bursting with sweet and refreshing flavors. Whether enjoyed fresh, as raisins, or in wine, they offer a delightful taste experience.

Pomegranates

Pomegranates are regal orbs filled with ruby-red arils, offering a delightful balance of sweet and tangy flavors. Their rich, antioxidant-packed seeds make for a healthy and exotic addition to your fruit basket.

Strawberries

Strawberries are vibrant, heart-shaped delights, celebrated for their juicy, sweet-tart taste and vibrant red hue. They're a quintessential summer treat, perfect for snacking, baking, or adding a burst of freshness to your salads and desserts.
